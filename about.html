About Me

Chandler M. Gerdes

At the grand age of 21 years I am both a student and a salesman. I am currently going to Bellevue College and have been for about a year now getting my prerequisites finished up so that I can transfer to the University of Washington and go into their I.M.D. program and eventually graduate from there. While I have been going to school I have also been working at a few different places, I was working at Microsoft for a while and then more recently I started working as a salesman for Seattle Lighting in Bellevue.

After finishing college I hope to move and work for a large company in a design and development role and/ or an advisory role into the world of human centric media design and more aesthetically/ user pleasing UX UI. I hope to also continue to work on my own projects. Maybe eventually developing a full experience project entirely my own.
